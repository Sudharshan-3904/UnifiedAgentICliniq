# Instructions for Making Filter Coffee
- Boil water in a pot.
- Place a coffee filter inside the filter basket of your coffee maker.
- Grind medium-coarse coffee beans to a consistency similar to sand.
- Add 1-2 tablespoons of ground coffee per cup of desired strength.
- Slowly pour hot water over the coffee grounds, allowing them to steep for about 4 minutes.
- After 4 minutes, gently press down on the filter to extract the coffee. Pour into cups and enjoy!
